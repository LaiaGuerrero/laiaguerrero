Thinking by heart and designing by brain

My design process is a flexible journey that emphasizes collaboration and continuous improvement. Throughout, I leverage methodologies like Design Thinking and Lean UX to ensure efficiency and user-centered outcomes.

1. The Challenge

Kick off with the briefing.

Clearly articulate the problem and project goals—no guesswork allowed.

2. Understand

Let the heart lead.

Empathize with users, research the context, and uncover insights.

3. Define

Time for the brain to shine.

Extract findings, identify opportunities, and set design direction—what and why.

4. Ideate

Creativity unleashed.

Explore ideas through sketches, moodboards, and quick prototypes.

5. Iterate

Fail fast, learn fast.

Build, test, and refine ideas based on real feedback before final design.

6. Final Design

Where brain and heart converge.

Polish the solution, prepare assets, and ensure a smooth handoff.
