My design process

My design process is a flexible, iterative journey that emphasizes collaboration and continuous improvement. It begins with Understand, where I research to define the problem. In the Define phase, I set clear objectives and strategies. I then move to Ideate, exploring creative solutions, followed by Test, where I validate and refine concepts. Finally, in the Final Design stage, I deliver a polished solution. Throughout, I leverage methodologies like Design Thinking and Lean UX to ensure efficiency and user-centered outcomes.
