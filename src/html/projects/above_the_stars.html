THE CHALLENGE

How might we create a visually compelling and cohesive identity for CND’s annual distributor convention in Athens?

CND’s EMEA Distributors Conference brings gathers 130 key partners to strengthen collaboration and boost sales. However, previous editions lacked engagemen due to inconsistent branding, unclear messaging, and a weak connection to the event’s location. To elevate the experience, we needed a captivating concept would resonate with an international audience while staying true to CND’s values. The challenge was to design an identity that conveyed exclusivity, celebration, and the timeless grandeur of Athens, making attendees feel part of something truly extraordinary.

THE SOLUTION

A visual identity inspired by astrology—an integral part of Ancient Greek culture—as a metaphor for strategic vision and decision-making. This theme infused every touchpoint, creating a seamless and immersive brand experience.

Every design decision was driven by three core principles:

Celestial creative concept

The event became a cosmic journey, linking Greek astrology to CND’s vision of strategic decision-making and business growth.

Personalised experience

Custom name badges, tailored materials, and interactive elements fostered a deeper connection with attendees.

Design coherence

A premium, refined aesthetic ensured a cohesive experience across all touchpoints, from signage to digital presentations.

DESIGN PROCESS

This project blended research, creativity, and iteration to develop a brand identity that truly resonated with CND’s audience. Working closely with the event’s project manager, I aligned each phase with the client's strategic vision and real user feedback.

Read about our journey of turning insights into impactful solutions:

1. Understand

Interviews with five attendees revealed a need for immersive yet clear branding. Research into Ancient Greece led to three conceptual directions, with astrology emerging as the most engaging and universally relevant theme.

2. Define

Targeted high-net-worth CND distributors (ages 30–60) seeking exclusivity and professional growth. The design needs to reflect collective ambition, sophistication, and strategic foresight.

3. Ideate

Created moodboards with celestial themes, tested color palettes, and refined the logo to balance mysticism with modernity, ensuring legibility and creativity across all applications.

4. Iterate

20 one-on-one sessions with potential participants provided real-time feedback, allowing me to refine the digital and print materials to improve interactivity and thematic consistency across all design assets.

FINAL DESIGN

The completed identity seamlessly integrated across all event materials—from the logo and digital presentations to signage, merchandise, and hospitality desks. The result was a visually striking brand experience that elevated the convention’s impact and left a lasting impression on attendees.

LEARNINGS

From concept to experience: crafting meaningful event branding

Transforming astrology into a brand identity required balancing symbolism with clarity. Each design choice—from celestial motifs to interactive kits—enhanced immersion. The challenge was to evoke mystique while maintaining accessibility. Seeing attendees deeply connect with the theme reinforced the power of thoughtful storytelling.

Sophie, Event Attendee: The branding felt truly immersive—every detail made us feel part of something special, like we were stepping into another world. It wasn’t just an event; it was an experience.

Bridging strategy and aesthetics across touchpoints

Ensuring a seamless experience in both digital and print materials reinforced my ability to apply one design to different applications, each piece had to be distinct yet interconnected. This project deepened my capacity to maintain brand consistency without sacrificing usability, proving that a strategic mindset is essential when designing a brand identity.

Ana, CND Marketing Lead: Above the Stars branding elevated the event—attendees engaged with the materials effortlessly, and the cohesive visuals strengthened our key messaging. The attention to detail truly made a difference.

Iterate, adapt, elevate: the power of feedback

Testing the materials with real users revealed key insights into how they interacted with the brand. Small refinements—like adjusting contrast in presentations or refining typography—significantly improved usability. The result? A 92% attendee approval rate and a branding strategy that truly resonated. Great design isn’t just about aesthetics but about how people experience it.
