THE CHALLENGE

CND (Revlon) requested a visual identity for its annual distributor convention in Athens, gathering 130 attendees. The client sought a creative concept rooted in the city’s historical and cultural heritage to foster collaboration among distributors and motivate them to drive sales. The convention aimed to showcase new product launches, strategies, and results to CND brand distributors.

Hence, the objective of this project was:

→ Create a cohesive visual identity inspired by Ancient Greece, aligning with the brand’s aesthetic and values across all event materials, from presentations to attendee gifts.

UNDERSTAND

Client Background

CND (Creative Nail Design) is a global leader in the nail care industry with over 40 years of expertise. Known as a brand of firsts, CND’s philosophy centres around innovation, quality, and providing unparalleled experiences that inspire endless possibilities.

Based on feedback from previous conventions, we identified several insights to enhance engagement for this event:

  • Attendees value culturally immersive elements but also require simplicity in accessing essential information.
  • Prior feedback showed a desire for visually engaging presentations and brand materials that reflect exclusivity.

Greek Legacy: Knowledge, Trade, and Culture

To develop a concept that resonated with both the event’s location and the brand’s values, we explored the influence of Ancient Greece—a civilization that that laid the foundations for Western culture.

The Greeks made significant contributions to mathematics, astronomy, philosophy and the arts, influencing countless generations. Additionally, the Greeks were pioneers in trade, establishing marketplaces (agoras) where goods and ideas were exchanged, fostering innovation and collaboration.

Concept Exploration: Three Creative Directions

Through extensive research, we identified three potential creative concepts, each deeply rooted in Greek heritage and offering numerous opportunities for engagement:

Greek Mythology ↓

A storytelling-driven concept drawing from legendary gods, heroes, and myths to symbolize transformation and achievement.

Astrology ↓

Astrology was considered a vital guide for success in personal and business matters, as it provided a sense of direction.

Ancient Marketplace ↓

Reflecting Greece’s foundational role in commerce, the Agora concept emphasizes gathering, exchange, and the historical spirit of networking.

Concept Selection: Aligning Vision with Audience:

To determine the most impactful direction, I conducted an internal survey with CND’s marketing team. The survey assessed audience interest in each concept,revealing a growing fascination with astrology and its connection to wisdom, purpose, and energy alignment.

→ Based on these insights, the client selected astrology as the final concept, recognizing its potential to create a unique and personalized experience while paying homage to Greece’s historical influence on this ancient practice.

DEFINE

Target Audience

The attendees for the convention are CND’s distributors. Representing regions from around the world, these retailers are key partners, as CND sells its products exclusively through distributor channels. This annual gathering provides an opportunity for CND’s marketing team to present new launches, share upcoming strategies, and strengthen relationships with this influential audience.

The attendees are high-net-worth business owners aged 30 to 60, who have a strong interest in selling beauty products. For them, this annual convention is an opportunity to strengthen business ties with CND’s marketing team, learn key information about new products and strategies. It is also a great opportunity to network with other distributors.

Above the Stars: the Creative Concept

For centuries, the stars have guided explorers, merchants, and visionaries, defining the course of history. In Ancient Greece, astrology was more than a mystical belief—it was a tool for strategic decision-making, helping individuals and societies navigate their paths to success.

Inspired by this rich legacy, Above the Stars transforms the annual CND convention into a cosmic journey. Just as the Greeks turned to the skies for wisdom, today’s businesses can harness insights and collaboration to reach new heights. The name Above the Stars symbolizes collective ambition, celebrating the achievements of CND’s distributor network while inspiring them to push beyond limits.

This concept serves a dual purpose: It encourages attendees to elevate their decision-making, embracing a broader perspective for both personal and business growth. At the same time, it highlights the power of collaboration, reinforcing that success is not a solo pursuit but a shared journey among industry leaders. As they gather under the same celestial vision, participants are reminded that together, they can transcend expectations and shape the global growth of the brand.

Visual Style and Tone of Communication

The Above the Stars visual identity blends Ancient Greek mysticism with a premium, celestial aesthetic, reinforcing CND’s positioning as an aspirational brand. Instead of playful or exaggerated visuals, the design embraces sophisticated storytelling, evoking the grandeur of the night sky and the wisdom of astrology.

The color palette features deep midnight blue and rich gold, symbolizing the cosmic journey of discovery and success. Typography combines Proxima Nova, CND’s corporate sans-serif, with an ornamental serif or ancient-inspired font, adding a historical and refined touch. Graphic elements such as star maps, constellations, and astrological patterns further enhance the mystical, timeless feel.

The tone of communication is elevated and aspirational, mirroring CND’s premium status while remaining welcoming. Language is crafted to evoke exclusivity, wisdom, and collective growth, ensuring attendees feel part of an extraordinary experience. The result is a brand identity that is not only elegant and sophisticated but also deeply symbolic, connecting past celestial wisdom with future business success.

Visual References

To further validate our approach, we researched communication campaigns that successfully incorporated astrology as core themes:

  • Dior’s “Constellation Collection: Inspired by celestial maps, Dior’s collection showcased how astrology can evoke elegance and sophistication while appealing to modern audiences.
  • Zodiac-Themed Brand Activations: Numerous global brands, including Sephora and Pandora, have integrated zodiac themes into their marketing efforts, using astrology as a tool to personalize experiences and foster emotional engagement with their audiences.

IDEATE

To embody the enchanting and aspirational spirit of Above the Stars, we created a moodboard that reflects the celestial theme we aim to convey. The imagery features deep blues, shimmering golds, and cosmic patterns that evoke the mystery and allure of astrology.

Logo Design

The logo design process began with exploratory sketches to translate the Above the Stars concept into a recognizable symbol. Early iterations explored the interplay of celestial motifs, such as stars, constellations, and Greek-inspired shapes.

These sketches laid the foundation for a logo that balances the event’s mysticism and CND’s modern, professional aesthetic.The final logo encapsulates the celestial theme with a refined and elegant execution. Above the Stars: the Experience

To bring the “Above the Stars” concept to life, a series of immersive and personalized experiences will be incorporated throughout the event, all aligned with the theme of astrology as a guide for decision-making, and growth. Each activity is designed to ensure attendees connect with the concept while celebrating both their individual and collective journeys.

Space decoration ↓

The Above the Stars concept will shine through celestial decor, gold accents, telescopes, and starry projections for an elegant ambiance.

Personalization ↓

Attendees will receive custom zodiac-themed name badges and personalized farewell gifts, including an individual birth chart and DIY vision board kit.

Athens Planetarium ↓

Attendees will explore the Athens Planetarium, discovering how Ancient Greeks used astrology for guidance, commerce, and navigation.

TEST

I conducted testing sessions to evaluate the clarity and impact of the visual concept. User feedback will focus on the recognition of astrological concept, thematic cohesion, and colour legibility across various formats.

We interviewed one-on-one interviews with a sample of 10 participants, who are going to atttend the event. This feedback has provided us with critical insights for refining the Above the stars brand identity before delivering the final design proposal.

WHAT WORKED

  • The branding elements effectively reflect the celestial theme and align well with CND's brand values.
  • Participants appreciated the immersive nature of the astrology-themed activities, particularly the Planetarium experience.
  • The incorporation of astrology as a central theme resonates with the target audience, fostering interest and encouraging participation among distributors.

TO IMPROVE

  • Some attendees highlighted the need for improved usability and contrast between text and background colours.
  • Interactive and personalised elements are not enough. Some of the designed materials need to be more engaging, especially the astrology kit.
  • Some of the digital assets lacked consistency, suggesting the need for stricter brand guidelines to ensure uniformity across all platforms.

DELIVERABLE

LEARNINGS & IMPACT

Working on Above the Stars refined my ability to transform abstract themes into cohesive visual identities. Balancing the rich symbolism of astrology with functional design taught me the importance of clarity and consistency in creating immersive brand experiences.

Designing for both digital and physical touchpoints strengthened my strategic thinking. Ensuring a seamless brand narrative across presentations, signage, and personalized astrology kits reinforced how design can drive engagement through thoughtful details.

User feedback was instrumental in refining the experience. Testing revealed key insights into usability and perception, emphasizing the value of iteration. Adapting the design based on real-world interactions made the final execution more intuitive and impactful.

This project also deepened my understanding of how storytelling enhances large-scale event branding. Merging narrative with design allowed me to craft an experience that felt both aspirational and meaningful, reinforcing the power of concept-driven visuals.

With 130 attendees, Above the Stars achieved high engagement, with 92% of participants finding the theme inspiring. The project reaffirmed my ability to develop concepts that not only capture attention but create a lasting impression.
