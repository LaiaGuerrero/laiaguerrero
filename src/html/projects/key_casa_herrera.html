THE KEY TO CASA HERRERA

a curated registration website for the Casa Herrera Seminar

Overview

Imagine the Casa Herrera Seminar website as the key to an exclusive beauty experience. This responsive digital hub guides international attendees through a refined registration journey, offering essential event details, a VIP-style welcome, and curated local insights to unlock everything the seminar and Barcelona have to offer.

Final Design

Outcomes & Impact

30% Faster Registration: Streamlined user flow reduced registration time by 30% compared to last year’s website.

Improved Conversion: Intuitive steps and clear visual cues boosted the confirmation rate by simplifying each stage.

Enhanced User Experience: Responsive design and curated local tips dramatically improved usability for busy international guests.

The Challenge The Solution Design Process Final Design Learnings

THE CHALLENGE

How might we design a digital experience that unlocks an elegant, intuitive registration process and curated local insights for Casa Herrera Seminar’s international attendees?

Previous event sites suffered from poor mobile optimization, flat content, and a lack of relevant local guidance. With guests ranging from makeup artists to marketing leads—and most registering via smartphone—we needed an elegant yet practical solution that truly felt like their personal key to Casa Herrera Seminar.

THE SOLUTION

Every design decision was driven by three core principles:

Mobile-first approach

With 68% of last year’s visitors accessing the site via mobile, designing for small screens was non-negotiable.

Experience Barcelona page

Inspired by Instagram stories, this interactive section lets users swipe through luxury experiences in Barcelona filtered to their interests.

The key concept

Upon registration, attendees receive a digital key that unlocks new sections of the site—reinforcing a sense of exclusive access to Casa Herrera.

DESIGN PROCESS

To create a digital experience worthy of the Casa Herrera Seminar, I followed a user-centered and iterative design approach. Each phase—from research to refinement—was guided by the goal of turning registration into a seamless, on-brand journey that feels like receiving a personal key to an exclusive event.

Read about our journey of turning insights into impactful solutions:

1. Understand

Interviewed 20 past and potential attendees to uncover key pain points: clunky mobile forms, lack of local recommendations, and a generic registration flow that failed to reflect the brand.

2. Define

Immersed in the brand’s identity, I defined an experiential direction, mapped content priorities, and identified moments where the experience could feel more personal.

3. Ideate

Developed user personas, sketched storyboards for the key journey, and created low and high-fidelity prototypes in Figma—carefully balancing utility with visual elegance.

4. Iterate

Ran one-on-one usability tests with 10 users across different devices. Each iteration improved clarity, flow, and emotional impact—bringing the product closer to a seamless, on-brand premium experience.

FINAL DESIGN

After integrating feedback and polishing the prototype, I delivered the final responsive design alongside clear documentation, assets, and dev-ready guidelines. The finished site is fully responsive. Every asset—from iconography to micro-animations—reinforces the high-end brand while ensuring that busy international guests can register and discover curated recommendations in just a few intuitive taps.

LEARNINGS

Bridging elegance & functionality

Balancing high-end aesthetics with smooth usability was key. A mobile-first mindset proved that luxury design and performance can coexist. Visual clarity, strong hierarchy, and subtle motion preserved brand prestige while ensuring an intuitive, fast, and elegant user experience.

Olivia, Event attendee: The website felt just like the brand—refined, intuitive, and truly welcoming. It made the whole process feel like part of the experience.

Power of experiential content

Introducing a swipeable, stories-style page turned the event site into an immersive experience. Guests explored curated recommendations in a personal, visual way—boosting engagement and emotionally connecting them to the brand before the event even began.

Ricard, CH Education Manager: This is the first time our event site has gone beyond logistics. It really reflects the spirit of Casa Herrera and gave our guests something memorable before they even arrived.

Cross-team collaboration & precision in handoff

Working closely with developers and PMs underscored the value of clear documentation, open communication and early technical alignment. This ensured the final product stayed true to the vision, while strengthening my ability to communicate across disciplines and anticipate constraints.
