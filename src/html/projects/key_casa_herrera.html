THE CHALLENGE

How might we design a registration website for Casa Herrera Seminar that balances luxury branding with seamless usability?

Casa Herrera Seminar is an exclusive training event by Carolina Herrera, set in the architecturally stunning Espai Corberó. However, past event websites struggled with mobile usability, unclear communication, and insufficient local information for international guests. The challenge was to craft an elegant yet practical digital experience—one that aligned with Carolina Herrera’s sophisticated identity while ensuring a smooth and engaging registration process for attendees worldwide.

THE SOLUTION

Every design decision was driven by three core principles:

Mobile-first approach

With 68% of last year’s visitors accessing the site via mobile, designing for small screens was non-negotiable.

VIP invitation

I crafted a registration flow that felt like receiving a personal invitation with a “Welcome Pass” and tailored local recommendations.

Elegant minimalism

I ensured that every visual element—from typography to interactive cues—reinforced a luxurious yet user-friendly experience.

DESIGN PROCESS

My design process is a flexible journey that emphasizes collaboration and continuous improvement. Throughout, I leverage methodologies like Design Thinking and Lean UX to ensure efficiency and user-centered outcomes.

Read about our journey of turning insights into impactful solutions:

1. Understand

Conducted surveys and interviews with 20 potential users to uncover pain points such as mobile usability issues and the need for clear, accessible event information.

2. Define

Immersed in Carolina Herrera’s aesthetic guidelines, we identified key design elements that echoed the brand’s sophistication and the event venue’s architectural beauty.

3. Ideate

Developed user personas and mapped out the registration journey as a “digital key” to the event. Created sketch wireframes and iterated through low- and high-fidelity prototypes in Figma.

4. Iterate

Engaged 10 users in one-on-one sessions to refine the navigation flow and ensure the design resonated across devices.

FINAL DESIGN

After incorporating user feedback and refining the prototype, I prepared the final design and all necessary guidelines, assets, and documentation to ensure a smooth handover. The design was fully responsive, with careful attention to typography, layout, and interactive elements to ensure the best user experience across all platforms.

LEARNINGS

Bridging elegance & functionality

Balancing Carolina Herrera’s high-end aesthetics with a seamless user experience was a defining challenge. A mobile-first approach ensured accessibility without compromising the brand’s refined identity. This experience reinforced my ability to prioritize clarity, hierarchy, and usability while maintaining a visually compelling design.

The registration process was smooth and beautifully designed—it felt premium, just like the brand.

- Hayley, Event Attendee

Refining the registration flow

Testing multiple user flows for registration underscored the impact of small optimizations on conversion rates. Each iteration fine-tuned the process, ensuring users could complete their registration effortlessly and intuitively.

Cross-team collaboration & precision in handoff

Aligning with project managers and developers highlighted the importance of clear documentation and structured design handoff. Ensuring design intent was preserved through implementation strengthened my ability to communicate effectively across disciplines and anticipate technical constraints early in the process.
